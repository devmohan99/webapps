Why you’ll love Wagestream

Stream wages

Your pay, your way

Wagestream lets employees stream a percentage of the money that they've already earned into their account in seconds. So you have instant access to your pay throughout the month - whenever you need it!

Track wages

Track your earnings in real time

Many of us don't know exactly what we are going to earn until payday rolls around. That makes it hard, if not impossible, to budget and plan ahead. Wagestream lets you track your earnings throughout the month - so you can always stay on top of your money.

Save wages

Saving is simple  with Safestream

We all know it's important to have a rainy day fund, but some things are easier said than done. With Safestream you can automatically set aside a small amount from your salary each month.
And even better - Wagestream will double the contribution of randomly selected employees every month.

Learn skills

Financial education for  better financial health

How we feel about money depends on the choices we make every day.
We’ve created a financial education programme, in conjunction with The Money Charity and Money Advice Service to help you improve your money management. From cash flow and budgeting advice to understanding a payslip – you’ll find a library of expert tools, tips and articles on the Wagestream app.

Change your lifestyle with Financial wellness

Everyone pays when employees feel financially stressed - that's why we've built a financial wellness programme to empower everyone to make smarter, healthier choices based around five easy steps.
